Privacy & Security

  • No data is sent to CodeTweak servers
  • All requests go directly to your chosen AI provider

Getting Started

  • Obtain an API key from your chosen provider
  • OpenAI: platform.openai.com
  • Anthropic: console.anthropic.com
  • Google Gemini: aistudio.google.com
  • For Gemini: Select "Google (Gemini)" provider and your model - the endpoint will be configured automatically
